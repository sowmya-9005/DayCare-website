Our Childcare Programs

Infant Care Program

Ages: 12 months - 28 months

Our nurturing infant program provides a safe, loving environment where your baby can grow and explore with individualized attention from our certified caregivers.

✓ 1:3 caregiver-to-infant ratio
✓ Daily developmental activities
✓ Individualized feeding & sleeping schedules
✓ Regular communication with parents

Sample Daily Schedule

7:00-8:30 AM Arrival & feeding
8:30-9:30 AM Tummy time & sensory play
9:30-11:00 AM Morning nap
11:00 AM-12:00 PM Outdoor time (weather permitting)

Toddler Discovery Program

Ages: 3years - 4 years

Our toddler program encourages exploration and independence through structured play, early social skills development, and hands-on learning experiences.

✓ Art & music exploration

Preschool Readiness Program

Ages: 5 - 6 years

Our preschool program prepares children for kindergarten through a balanced curriculum of academic readiness, social-emotional learning, and creative expression.

✓ Early literacy & math skills
✓ Social skills development
✓ Weekly themed units
